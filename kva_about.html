Hi, I'm Valerie

I'm a fine artist and
a web design/development student

My work is based on a realistic art style, most often achieved through colored pencil.
Other mediums I practice with include oil paints, watercolor, and digital art.
I have been commissioned over 40 pieces, ranging from family or pet portraits to landscapes and still lives.

Ever since I was a little girl, I have found an outlet for self-expression through drawing. Whether in class
or at home, I would observe objects of my surroundings and try to capture their shape, shading,
and appearance from my perspective. Being a great admirer of nature, living subjects such as
people and animals draw my attention. Art is not my only hobby, however. I fill my days with friends,
family, worship to God, and travel. The beauty in all of these parts of my life inspire my art.
